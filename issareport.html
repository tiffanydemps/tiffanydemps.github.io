Pronounced: It's A Report

Term "ISSA" Orgin: As defined by Urbandictionary: Issa is a slang term highly popularized by rapper, 21 savage. While describing his forehead tattoo in an interview 21 used "Issa" to say "it's a". Relevance: Issareport is the perfect name for my reporter personality. I wanted something cool, fun, catchy yet traditional. The best way to describe what I was doing was to simply say: Hey guys, watch my videos "it's a report". As silly as it sounds, it just stuck and I love it. I strive to bring diversity to the world of Broadcast Journalism. I absolutely love to change my hair and switch up my look, I believe the reporting world needs more of this!
